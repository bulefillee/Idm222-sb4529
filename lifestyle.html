2023 Spring/Summer Essentials On Your Radar

FLORAL POWER

 

Spotted at: (L-R) Loewe, Richard Quinn & Roksanda. As spring unlocks the flowers, the floral season begins, get ready for floral vibes, everything! Yes, the timeless trend continues to grow, as the top labels from London Fashion week hit us with these fresh vibes!

BLUE HUES

 

Spotted at: (L-R) David Koma, Cecilie Bahnsen & Alexander McQueen. As the blue hue takes center stage, the runway caught splashes with cobalt &baby blue hues that were also resolutely fluid and innocent at Prada, Burberry & Emporio Armani.

FRINGING

 

Spotted at: (L-R) Jil Sander, Victoria Beckham & Proenza Schouler. As a classically chic ensemble equipped with the accent of details, from fringed 'cuffs' or fringed dresses to fringed clutch bags, it's everything fringe indeed!

GOTH 2.0

 

Spotted at: (L-R)Paco Rabanne, Dior, Dolce & Gabbana. An unlikely shade brings a significant moment in the spring/summer landscape. The color black fused with soft grunge aesthetic, the theme of the latest runways giving the looks an unanticipated softness!
